About
Hind Public Higher Secondary School is established as a quality co-educational school, which has is affiliated to the Chhattisgarh Board of Secondary Education, Raipur.

Hind Public School covers a full span of schooling state, starting from Nursery to Class-VIII in English Medium and from Class I to Class XII in Hindi Medium. It is a fully child-oriented school and ensures "holistic development" of the students' mental and physical potential. A spiritual current flows in all and an educational process serves to make the student grow as an adult equipped with values based deeply in the country's rich cultural traditions.
