Admissions
  • Following documents will be required during the time of admission:
    • Attested Copy of the Birth Certificate issued by Municipal Corporation
    • Transfer Certificate issued by the previous school
    • Caste Certificate (only for SC/ST/OBC)
    • AADHAR Card
    • Bank Pass-Book
  • Students will be admitted on the basis of a written test and a formal interview
  • Students taking admission from Class II to Class XI must submit orginal copy of Transfer Certificate (T.C.)
  • Students taking admission from states other than Chhattisgarh must provide signature of District Education Officer on their Transfer Certificate (T.C.)
About
Under the aegis of New Hind Education Society, Hind Public School was established in the year 1995-96 and founded on 10th August 1996. Hind Public Higher Secondary School has six strong missions:
  • To develop the spirits of national integration and create a sense of India among children.
  • To build character in enriching minds.
  • To purpose excellence and set the pace in the field of school education.
  • To strength knowledge for from the roots with different teaching ways.
  • To initiate and promote experimentation and innovations in education.
  • To give varied experience that lasts lifetime.
Hind Public School is based on a shared commitment to academic excellence, intellectual growth, art, athletics, high standards of ethical awareness, sportsmanship and community service. We have Nursery to Class IX in English Medium and Class I to Class XII in Hindi Medium classes. Our motivate is to shape the institute in a better way and committed for continuous improvement.
